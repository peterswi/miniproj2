Visualizations: Mini Project 2

Erin Corcoran, Lurein Perera, Will Peters & Kathryn Tatum

For our project, we decided to explore data on fatalities in police shootings in the United States since 2015. This data comes from the Washington Post's database on police shootings. Though the data is from 2015, these topics are still highly relevant today, particularly in the wake of George Floyd's murder in May.
