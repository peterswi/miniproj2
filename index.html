Visualizations: Mini Project 2

Erin Corcoran, Lurein Perera, Will Peters & Kathryn Tatum

For our project, we decided to explore data on hate crimes and police killings in the United States in 2015. Both datasets come from FiveThirtyEight's public datasets. Though the data is from 2015, these topics are still highly relevant today, particularly in the wake of George Floyd's murder in May.

This is an analysis of some hatecrimes.
